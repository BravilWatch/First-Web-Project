Downsides

  • If it is not properly secured, then data is vulnerable to loss or theft.
  • A third party may hijack your account/service.
  • The service or API may be insecure.
  • Your information is vulnerable to DDOS attacks.
  • Your provider may have downtime in order to do maintenance.
  • You have very limited control over the infrastructure.
